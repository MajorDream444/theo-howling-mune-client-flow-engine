# System Map

```text
Discovery
-> Front Door
-> Intake
-> Qualification
-> CRM
-> Close
-> Paid Client
-> Follow-up
-> Data Review
```

## Discovery

Prospects arrive through referrals, existing relationships, content CTAs, DMs, or direct sharing.

## Front Door

One landing page explains Howling Mune, the first offer path, fit/not-fit, and the application CTA.

## Intake

Application captures identity, contact, why now, current challenge, desired shift, pathway interest, format, budget comfort, and consent.

## Qualification

Lead is routed into healing-only, photography-only, hybrid identity reset, group/workshop, nurture, partner/retreat, or manual review.

## CRM

Airtable stores lead state, route, readiness, budget, next action, close probability, notes, and source.

## Close

WhatsApp/Telegram sequence or fit call leads to proposal/SOW, acceptance, and payment.

## Paid Client

Payment updates status and activates build/client tasks.

## Follow-up

Unclosed but aligned leads move into nurture or later follow-up.

## Data Review

Review lead sources, pathways, objections, close rates, and language patterns before expanding the system.
