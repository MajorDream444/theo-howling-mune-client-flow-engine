# Content Rhythm

## Principle

Content should create a clean signal, not performance pressure. Theo does not need a fame-seeking machine. The rhythm should protect depth and give aligned people a way to enter.

## Pillars

- self-relationship
- body and nervous system awareness
- silence and contemplation
- identity and visual rebirth
- relational repair
- group dynamics and men's work
- Howling Mune doctrine

## Cadence

- 1 primary reflection per week
- 1 short invitation or application CTA per week
- 1 proof/process note when real and approved
- monthly review of which language creates aligned applications

## Voice

Calm, direct, spacious, embodied, not salesy. Use plain language over mystical overstatement.

## CTA Rhythm

Every public post does not need a hard CTA. Use periodic invitations into the application path and a clear profile/site link as the stable front door.

## What Not To Do

- do not chase trends
- do not make healing guarantees
- do not post performative vulnerability for reach
- do not copy generic wellness language
- do not imply clinical or medical outcomes
