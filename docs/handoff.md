# Theo / Howling Mune Handoff

## Who Theo Is

Theo is the founder behind Howling Mune, a body of healing and creative work centered on self-relationship, embodiment, contemplation, group dynamics, and returning people to themselves. The known brand tagline is: "Giving you back to you."

Theo has used the phrase Somatic Alchemist. That phrase is useful source language, but it should not become final public positioning without human review.

## What Is Real Already

- Real lived authority and transformation.
- Existing 1:1 work.
- Group and men's work experience.
- Model/creative work and visual identity context.
- Relationship-centered healing work.
- A larger vision around groups, practitioners, retreats, healing facilities, and system-level expansion.

## Business Problem

The work has relied on referrals, word of mouth, scattered DMs, manual explanation, inconsistent pricing, and no clean front door. There is no single pathway for aligned prospects to understand, apply, get qualified, and move into paid engagement.

## Phase 1 Recommendation

Build the first client-flow engine:

- front door landing page
- intake/application form
- routing logic
- Airtable lead tracker
- close sequence
- Stripe milestone path
- launch checklist and review rhythm

## Offer Architecture

Howling Mune stays the umbrella brand. The first flagship offer is the first room opened inside the house. Existing strategy identified Identity Reset / Visual Rebirth as a possible flagship path, but final offer name, pricing, and public promise require human approval.

## What Should Not Be Built Yet

- Full creator-healer operating system.
- Retreat platform.
- Practitioner network.
- Corporate/facility expansion.
- Complex membership/community architecture.
- Automated pricing or proposal finalization.
- Broad social performance machine.

## Client Clarity OS Connection

Theo plugs into the generic OS loop:

```text
Client record -> Intake/source material -> Blueprint extraction -> Human review
-> Proposal/SOW -> Stripe payment -> Build tasks -> Front door/intake/CRM
-> Launch and optimization
```
