# Close Flow

## Channels

Primary close channels may be WhatsApp or Telegram, depending on Theo's current preference. Final channel choice requires confirmation.

## Sequence

1. Intake received.
2. Operator reviews route and fit.
3. Warm acknowledgment sent.
4. Voice note or message reflects back the client pattern.
5. Fit call offered when needed.
6. Proposal / offer summary sent.
7. One-page SOW sent.
8. Acceptance/signature collected.
9. Optional alignment checkbox selected only in writing if applicable.
10. Stripe Milestone 01 link sent.
11. Cash App backup used only if approved.
12. Signed PDF returned and first payment received.
13. Build begins within 48 hours.

## Voice Note Structure

- name what was heard
- reflect the "why now"
- name the likely path without overpromising
- invite the next step
- keep the tone grounded and direct

## Fit Call Logic

Use a fit call for high-value, unclear, sensitive, partner, or manual-review leads. Avoid turning every lead into an unpaid exploratory drain.

## Objection Handling

- Price: hold value, do not negotiate automatically.
- Timing: clarify readiness and next window.
- Unclear fit: route to nurture or manual review.
- Skepticism: point to process and application, not exaggerated claims.

## Acceptance Path

Proposal/SOW and pricing require human approval. Optional 10% system-generated revenue alignment for 6 months may be included only if selected in writing.
