# Founder Profile

## Identity

Theo is the founder of Howling Mune. The work sits at the intersection of healing, embodiment, self-relationship, contemplation, group dynamics, and creative/visual identity.

## Authority

Known authority comes from lived transformation and work already happening across 1:1 support, group work, men's work, creative/model work, and relationship-centered healing.

## Resistance

Theo does not want performative social media or empty fame-seeking visibility. The system must create a clean pathway without diluting the essence of the work into shallow content or exaggerated public claims.

## Vision

Long-term vision may include groups, practitioners, retreats, healing facilities, and larger systems. V1 does not build all of that. V1 creates the first reliable pathway around the work.

## Language

- Howling Mune
- Giving you back to you
- Somatic Alchemist
- self-relationship
- physical / metaphysical / ego layers
- silence and contemplation
- returning people to themselves

## What Must Be Protected

- The depth and integrity of the work.
- The distinction between real healing and performance.
- The umbrella brand.
- Human approval over pricing, claims, and public-facing strategy.
- The boundary between first revenue layer and long-term ecosystem vision.
